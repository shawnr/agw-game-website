✦ Your New Career Awaits ✦

You've just been hired at AG&W Inc. — the municipal utility company responsible for delivering mana of all types: arcana, gas, fire, earth, and water to every town, tower, dungeon, and dragon's lair in the region.

Your job is to lay the ley lines, build pipe networks, and route the resources to the right customers. Nobody thanks you when things work. Everyone blames you when they don't.

It's Mini Metro meets a magical cable company. Meditative when things flow. Chaotic when they don't. (It's mostly chaotic.)

✦ What You'll Do ✦

Route Magical Infrastructure

Connect sources to consumers across hex-grid maps. Manage 5 mana types. Build relays, splitters, transformers, batteries, and filters. Watch your network glow.

Solve Puzzles With Consequences

Balance budgets while keeping customers satisfied. Handle complaints from wizards, dragons, and dungeon lords. Some mana combos help — some explode.

Climb the Corporate Ladder

Start as a New Employee, rise to Chief of Reclamation. 19 hand-crafted campaign scenarios across 6 career tiers. Discover dark secrets in the company's past.

Retro-Futuristic Charm

CRT terminal aesthetic with phosphor glow and scanlines. AmigaOS-inspired desktop. WynnAmp music player. Satirical corporate newsfeed. A vibe.
